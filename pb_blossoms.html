Peanut Butter Blossoms

Yields: 2 dozen cookies

Time: 1 hour

Materials:

  • large mixing bowl
  • electric mixer
  • wooden spoon
  • sheet pan
  • ungreased cookie sheets
  • cooling rack

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cups softened butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • extra granulated sugar for rolling
  • 36 Hershey's Kisses

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190.6°C)
  2. In large bowl, beat 1/2 cup granulated sugar, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup peanut butter, 1/2 cup butter, and egg with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended
  3. Stir in flour, baking soda and baking powder
  4. Shape dough into 1-inch balls and roll each in additional granulated sugar.
  5. On ungreased cookie sheets, place the balls of dough about 2 inches apart
  6. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until edges are light golden brown
  7. Immediately press 1 milk chocolate candy in center of each cookie.
  8. Remove from cookie sheets to cooling rack.
*Adapted from Betty Crocker's Classic Peanut Butter Blossom Cookies
